## §A — Problem Statement

The central question is: does the (27̄,6) component of the 3875, via its symmetric Yukawa coupling to 27_i × 27_j, generate off-diagonal mixing with the correct Cabibbo-scale suppression? And can the full CKM matrix (V_us, V_cb, V_ub) be reproduced using RS bulk profile differences between generations?

**Success condition:** Exact CG coefficient computed; V_us predicted within ~20% of 0.225; RS profile analysis gives V_cb and V_ub in the correct ballpark. KC-g cleared (partial → no kill).

**Failure condition:** CG coefficient gives V_us ≪ 0.1 or ≫ 0.5; or (27̄,6) coupling structure is forbidden by some symmetry argument. KC-g becomes full kill → theory fails on flavor physics.
